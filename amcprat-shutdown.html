Discontinuing amcprat and replacing with a new basic tool

TLDR: I plan on discontinuing amcprat due to copyright concerns. This applies to amcprat and amcprat-simple.

The copyright concerns

Basically:

  • AMCPRAT is licensed under the MIT License
  • AMCPRAT-Simple is licensed under the GPL v3 License
  • The AMC/AIME problems are copyrighted, and the two projects contain these problems.
  • The HTML code that displays the problems is likely copyrighted by Art of Problem Solving.

These two projects contain copyrighted material, and that is the reason for shutting them down.

The future

AMCGEN is the new tool. It basically is amcprat-simple, but it provides AoPS wiki links instead. Check it out in one of the following places:

  • Github Pages
  • Gitlab Pages
  • Codeberg Pages
